<b><i>Relation to Originals</i></b>
<br>

In this Appendix, we provide logical facts which are inspired or implied by Newton's Principia and Euclid's Elements.
<br><br>

⦁  We do not do any claims these facts are known to Newton or Euclid. We do not insist on what Newton or Euclid intended to say. This may be discussed in Addendums under original Lemma or Proposition.
<br><br>

⦁  References to original text lemma are in parenthesises.
For example,

<a target="_blank" href="?conf=sappId=addd-prel-curveXX,theorionId=proof,aspectId=curve,subessayId=areas#theorem-2">"Lemma (Theorem II)"</a>

refers to

<a target="_blank" href="?conf=sappId=b1s2prop2theor2,theorionId=proof,aspectId=english">"Book 1. Section II. Proposition II. Theorem II".</a>

<b><i>Used axiomatic frameworks</i></b>
<br>
We use two frameworks, "Euclid" and "CalculusXX".
<a name="Eframework"></a>
<br><br><br><br>


<div style="background-color:#eeeeee;"><b>"Euclid"</b> framework<br></div>

derives Geometrical and Ratio
properties from "Euclid's Elements".

<a target="_blank" href="?conf=sappId=addd-prel">
Although real numbers are apparently logically follow from E.V.5</a>
and therefore are contained in Euclid framework, this framework pretends to ignore them
restricting itself with 
<a target="_blank" href="?conf=sappId=addd-prel#legacy-proportions">
"Legacy proportions and ratios"</a>,
which are interpreted as properties of geometrical objects or assumed as axioms for other objects like time or force.
<br><br>

Fundamental logical sequence: Geometry, ratio axioms, rational numbers --&gt; Geometry-based Calculus for magnitudes and possibly fluxions-based Calculus[1] for "numbers" --&gt; Physics

<a name="Cframework"></a>
<br><br><br><br>




<div style="background-color:#eeeeee;"><b>"CalculusXX"</b> framework<br></div>

is Calculus which is built in XIX-XX centuries.
<br><br>

Fundamental logical sequence: Axioms of Set theory --&gt; Number theory --&gt; Rational and Real numbers --&gt; the rest, Calculus, Analytic Geometry, ... --&gt; Physics
<br><br>

This sequence is more effective, than Geometry-based in Euclid framework.

<a target="_blank" href="?conf=sappId=addd-prel#legacy-proportions">
Legacy proportions</a>
are ignored in favor of concise Real number operations.
<br><br>
