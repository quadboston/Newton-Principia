*..*

If the given straight lines ¦AR¦¦ and ¦BR¦¦, together with the ¦arc-AB¦arc ACB¦¦,
its ¦AB¦chord AB¦¦,
and the ¦AD¦tangent AD¦¦, constitute three triangles
¦AR AB BR¦RAB¦¦, ¦AR BR arc-AB¦RACB¦¦, and ¦AR RD AD¦RAD¦¦, and if
then points ¦A¦¦ and ¦B¦¦ approach each other, I say that the triangles as they vanish
are similar in their ultimate form, and that their ultimate ratio is one of equality.
