For any monotonic and bounded function \( [A,E] \overset f \to \mathbb{R} \),

    \[
            \underset{\delta \to 0} {\text{lim }} U_\delta =
            \underset{\delta \to 0} {\text{lim }} L_\delta .
    \]

In plain words, circumscribed and inscribed sums converge to the same limit making upper and lower Darboux intergrals equal.
