Combinations of axes are defined as usual: <br>

ВҰDSВҰd<sub>+</sub>ВҰВҰ = a + c, <br>
G = ПҮВҰDHВҰd<sub>-</sub>ВҰВҰ = a - c, <br><br>

ПҮ = 1 for ellipse, -1 for hyperbola.
<br><br>

<br><br>

ВҰDSВҰd<sub>+</sub>ВҰВҰ ВҰDHВҰd<sub>-</sub>ВҰВҰ = ПҮ(aВІ - cВІ) = bВІ = a L / 2 =

<br><br>

( 4d<sub>+</sub> - ПҮ L ) / L = d<sub>+</sub> / d<sub>-</sub> <br><br>

or in Principia context <br><br>

( 4 DS - ПҮ L ) / L = DS / DH. <br><br>

When L grows from 0 to 4 DS, then, for ПҮ = 1, solution does exist and DH grows from 0 to oo,
and ellipse changes to a parabola.

After that, ПҮ must be -1 and conic transforms to a hyperbola.
